KBL is a savory stew that combines three main ingredients: kadyos (pigeon peas), baboy (pork), and langka (jackfruit). Each component brings its unique flavor and texture to the dish, creating a harmonious blend that is both hearty and satisfying.

Kadyos These legumes add a creamy texture and earthy flavor to the stew. They are a great source of protein and contribute to the dish's heartiness.

Baboy: Tender pieces of pork are typically simmered until they become juicy and flavorful. The pork adds richness to the broth, enhancing the overall taste of the dish.

Langka: The inclusion of ripe or unripe jackfruit provides a sweet contrast to the savory elements. When cooked, it becomes soft and tender, absorbing the flavors of the stew.

The dish is often seasoned with spices, herbs, and condiments such as ginger, garlic, and onions, which elevate its flavor profile. KBL is commonly enjoyed with steamed rice, making it a comforting meal that is perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.
